About Us

Decartem is a Software as a Service and an IOT startup that works in the field of Communication and Security. We were incorporated in 2019 and have business operations across India and the Middle east. We are incubated at Amity Innovation Incubator and have received support from Viridian Accelerator. Decartem is registered with MSME and is recognised under the Startup India Initiative.

Decartem offers a diverse product range starting from Millo (a free communication platform) to Attendo (A Student management system), Being a service provider we have a variety of digital branding solutions like Web Development, branding and more.

Our focus and objective is to provide a very meticulous, organised and professional service at the same time keep the clients finances happy! A tough combination this, but we ought to provide it no matter what!
